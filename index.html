Decolonized Future

The project aims to create a decolonized environment based on ideals of social justice, racial equality, and environmental responsibility. By engaging both looking to the future through speculative world-building exercises and looking to the past to recontextualize forgotten technology to suit modern needs, to envision a speculative decolonized urban future. During the project, our team explored different types of tools, such as MagicaVoxel and AR converter to build interactive 3D models. Inspiration: Lo-TEK Design by Radical Indigenism, by Julia Watson. Talking about the ideology of placing importance on local wisdom and indigenous innovation.
